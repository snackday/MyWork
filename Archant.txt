  A new kind of ant-spiders, found only on the most isolated caves of Tulimshar. 
  Able to melt the hardest iron by their acids, its recomended to wear strong 
  equipment in order to not get injured by their deadly acids
  According to several warriors, these monsters, sometimes carry some strange keys with them.
  There is a local legend, that speaks about a magical Sword, so hard it can't be melted even by the Archant acids. 
  It can be obtained by collecting these keys, but its unknown where to get one.
